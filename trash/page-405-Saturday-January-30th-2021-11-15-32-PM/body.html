Beer And Cider

George Saintsbury

How pleasant it is to find the famous Professor Saintsbury—known to students as the author of histories of the English and French literatures, the History of Criticism and _History of English Prosody_—spending the evening so hospitably in his cellar. I print this—from his downright delightful _Notes on a Cellar Book_—as a kind of tantalizing penance. It is a charming example of how pleasantly a great scholar can unbend on occasion.
